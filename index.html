About

Because of my musical background I come in to the field with experience traveling America and Europe with a small group of jazz musicians and teaching music camps at local schools. Why is that helpful for coding you ask? Well as of now I already posses many of the skills required for team building, out of the box thinking, colaberation and the ability to take ownership. Now I just have to add the languages. But this is where things get exciting for me, at the crossroads of coding and life experience. I can some it up in three ways.

  • Coding has always been something I never believed I could do and to be doing it now at my current level is a reward in of itself.
  • I love learning and to find out that there is no cap to what you can learn to do with coding is exciting!
  • Being able to work all over the world, learning from experienced programmers and all while making money to take care of myself and loved ones is a dream come true.

That is where Thinkful comes in. Thinkful has given me an oppertunity to learn without having to work outside of my courses. I am grateful for the chance to pursue certification through the Engineering immersion program.

Hopefully with time and effort I will be able to live and work in a way that supports my passions.
